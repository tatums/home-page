Our Story

It wasn't until they both moved home after college without jobs that they started dating while mooching off their parents, trying to get on their feet.

Many nights were spent trying every restaurant in Oak Park and at Healy's on Madison in Forest Park with friends. Kelly and Brian quickly became close, spending time together, meeting each other's friends and family, quoting the Simpsons and Kelly trying to convince Brian and the Shams that she should join the band with her tambourining expertise.
